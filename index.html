About

Let me introduce myself!

Hi, I'm Nav (Navpreet Matharu), a software developer with a knack for exploring my creative side and writing code to create memorable projects, as well as working in academia to help others do the same.

Profile

I studied Interactive Arts & Technology (major) and Computing Science (minor) at Simon Fraser University. I was immediately hooked on writing code from the first programming class I took.

I'm also constantly working on my creative skills— 3D modelling, animating, designing games, writing music, etc. and ultimately combining those things with my programming skills to develop games.

Beyond projects, I've worked as a co-op software developer at a few companies during my degree and I'm fond of working with students in academia.
